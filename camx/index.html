FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Does CamX support external USB cameras?

Yes, on iPad with USB-C. CamX supports UVC-compatible USB cameras, webcams, and HDMI capture devices on iPad running iPadOS 17 or later, with full control over format, resolution, and frame rate. iPhone does not support USB cameras.

Can I use my iPad as a portable capture monitor?

Yes. Connect a USB-C capture card to an iPad and CamX instantly turns it into a low-latency portable display — perfect for gaming, streaming, or filmmaking.

Are the video effects recorded into the file?

Yes. When VFX or picture adjustments are active, CamX bakes the effects into the recorded video and photos — not just the preview.

What iOS version is required?

CamX requires iOS 17.0 or later and runs on both iPhone and iPad. Photo capture via the volume button requires iOS 17.2+.
